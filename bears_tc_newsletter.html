Middle School Training Plan — This Week

Important — Optional Saturday Practice

This Saturday at 10:00 AM, Coach Zev and Coach Luke will meet any interested middle schoolers at the track for a workout (3 sets of [600m, 400m, 200m]). This will be the last practice we can host for middle schoolers for a while. With the CHSAA spring season officially starting, state rules do not allow us to have high school and middle school athletes at the same practices. Because we don’t have CHSAA practice this Saturday, we are thrilled to offer this one last MS session!

Also, Monument Academy track meets next week! If you have any questions about this transition or the workouts, please let me know.

For our middle schoolers keeping up their fitness, here is your training schedule for the week. Do +5 strides after every run.

Day Session
Monday Long run, 60 minutes. 12-minute warm-up and 12-minute cool-down.
Tuesday Easy 30–40 minute run.
Wednesday Workout! 12-minute warm-up, then head to the turf at Sanctuary Point. Do 10 × 1 lap at mile pace (pretty fast!) with 70 seconds rest between each lap. 12-minute cool-down.
Thursday Easy 30–40 minute run.
Friday Easy 30–40 minute run.
Saturday Optional Track Workout at 10:00 AM! See important note above.

Weekend Meet Recap

Huge congratulations to everyone who competed this past Saturday, as well as our athletes who ran at the CU Boulder meet on Friday! We had an unbelievable showing with Personal Records (PR) and Season Records (SR) dropping left and right:

Friday CU Boulder DMR Shoutouts

A massive congratulations to all our athletes who absolutely crushed the Distance Medley Relay (DMR) on Friday!

Boys DMR: Axel Mason, Lukas Sturgeon, Jake Bossinger, and Ben Olds.

Girls DMR: Zuri Troyer, Helen Ostrowski, Addison Michalak, Savannah Mitchell, Ellie McLellan, Naomi Hedstrand, and Esther Paulson.

Major props to Axel, Lukas, and Jake for doubling back to race again on Saturday. That is tough racing!

Saturday Boys Highlights

• Axel Mason ran a fantastic Season Best of 1:58.03 in the 800m!
• Elijah Parks absolutely crushed the 800m with a 2:10, which is insane! He also doubled back to run a 5:15 in the mile. Tough racing!
• Uriah Gaw had an incredible day—running a huge PR in the 800m and then coming back to run a 27.80 in the 200m. Amazing for a 7th grader!
• Carson Rodman had his best season opener ever in the 1600m (4:55.86).
• Kaleb Hanks walked away with an all-time PR in the 1600m (5:09.23)!
• Justin Saglembeni and Cameron Kraft both had huge days and massive PRs in the 800m.
• Josiah Lee stepped up big time in what was only his second-ever track meet—running both the 800m and the mile and setting a personal best in both!
• Preston Hall had a great season opener in the 1600m—lots more to come there!

Saturday Girls Highlights

• Chloe Allen and Claire Schneider absolutely crushed it in the 400m and 800m. They were some of the top middle schoolers at the entire meet!
• Kinley Hudson had a very busy day, taking on the 100m, 200m, and 400m and running beautifully.
• Macy Mikelson came through with a lifetime PR in her 1600m!
• Macie Shearer ran a fantastic 800m—great work out there!
• Livvy Haas ran a fantastic 5:26.59 in the 1600m! That is currently one of the top middle school times in the state of Colorado, and altitude-converted, one of the top times in the nation for her age group. Incredible!
